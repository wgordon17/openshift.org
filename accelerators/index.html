OpenShift Accelerators

Resources for open source innovators, evangelists, and community organizers

Accelerate Your Community

The Accelerators program is designed to enable, recognize, and reward successful users and promotors of the OpenShift platform.


Group members get:

  • Online access to monthly Accelerator team meetings
  • Support and advice from other Accelerators through our mailing list
  • Preview access to upcoming projects and initiatives
  • Tools, tips, examples, and expertise - helping you to share your OpenShift story in a compelling way

Accelerate the adoption of open source platforms in your community by becoming an active member in ours:

  • Share your slides, events, and CFPs with our mailing list and social media channels
  • Get increased exposure for your blog posts, videos, and screencasts
  • Discover opportunites to lead a talk or workshop, or to help organize local events
  • Help write, review, or translate OpenShift documentation
  • Adapt popular projects to run on an open cloud platform
  • Share your applications, demos, and service integrations

Application Process

Active team members may be eligible for material support (event sponsorship, training promotions, free stickers, etc)

  1. Watch the OpenShift project on GitHub
